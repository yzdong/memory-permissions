# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** 2024-01-15  
**Owner:** Legal & Compliance  
**Review Cycle:** Annual

## Purpose

This policy defines the acceptable use of company-owned or company-managed technology resources, including hardware, software, networks, and data. It applies to all employees, contractors, and vendors with access to our systems.

## Scope

- All endpoints (laptops, desktops, mobile devices)
- Internal networks and VPN connections
- Cloud services provisioned under the company account
- External SaaS tools approved via the software request process (see `../procurement/saas-approval-process.md`)

## Acceptable Use

- Use company resources primarily for business purposes
- Access only systems and data for which you have explicit authorization
- Report suspected security incidents to `security@company.internal` within 2 hours of discovery
- Store sensitive data only in approved locations (see `data-retention-schedule.md`)

## Prohibited Activities

- Installing unlicensed or unapproved software on company devices
- Sharing credentials or bypassing multi-factor authentication
- Using company infrastructure for personal financial gain or cryptocurrency mining
- Accessing, downloading, or distributing content that is illegal, offensive, or in violation of third-party IP rights
- Circumventing network monitoring or security controls
- Storing cardholder data outside PCI-scoped environments

## Personal Use

Incidental personal use is permitted provided it does not:
- Degrade system performance or network bandwidth
- Expose the company to legal or reputational risk
- Conflict with any other section of this policy

## Monitoring

The company reserves the right to monitor, log, and audit activity on company-owned systems. Employees should have no expectation of privacy on these systems.

## Violations

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination and referral to law enforcement where applicable.
