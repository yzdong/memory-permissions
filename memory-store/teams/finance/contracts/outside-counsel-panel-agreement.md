## Billing Guidelines

All panel firms must comply with the attached Outside Counsel Billing Guidelines (OCBG rev. 2025-01). Key requirements:

- No block billing — entries must be itemized in 0.1-hour increments
- No billing for first-year associate work without prior written approval
- Travel time billed at 50% of standard rate
- Expenses over $500 require pre-approval
- Monthly invoices due by the 10th of the following month; payment net-30

## Alternative Fee Arrangements (AFAs)

Tier 1 firms are eligible for AFAs on matters over $50K estimated fees. Structures used to date:
- Fixed fee for standard commercial contract review
- Success/contingency hybrid for IP enforcement matters

## Annual Review Process

Finance and Legal conduct joint review each November:
1. Matter spend by firm
2. Budget-to-actual variance by matter
3. Rate increase requests (cap: 5% YoY without CFO sign-off)
4. Performance feedback from internal stakeholders
