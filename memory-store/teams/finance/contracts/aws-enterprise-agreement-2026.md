## Summary

Three-year Enterprise Discount Program (EDP) agreement replacing the prior two-year EDP that expired 2025-12-31. Negotiated with AWS Enterprise team (account rep: Marcus Chen).

## Key Commercial Terms

- **Minimum commit:** $2.1M annually (billed monthly against actuals)
- **Discount tier:** 18% off on-demand rates across EC2, RDS, S3
- **Reserved Instance flexibility:** Convertible RIs included, 1-year and 3-year terms
- **Support plan:** Business Support included; upgrade to Enterprise Support at $15K/yr additional
- **SLA credits:** Standard AWS SLA applies; credit request window is 30 days post-incident

## Notable Redlines from Negotiation

- Removed AWS's standard limitation-of-liability cap that would have capped liability at 90 days of fees — replaced with 12-month cap
- Added data residency clause: all primary data stores must remain in `us-east-1` or `us-west-2`
- Negotiated out the automatic renewal clause; now requires 90-day written notice to renew
