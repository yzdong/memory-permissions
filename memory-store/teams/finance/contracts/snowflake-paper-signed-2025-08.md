## Deal Summary

Two-year capacity commitment for Snowflake Data Cloud. Negotiated during Snowflake's fiscal Q2 push — got meaningful concessions on pricing due to timing and our multi-year commitment signal.

## Commercial Terms

- **Annual credit commitment:** $480,000/year (paid upfront per year)
- **Credit unit price:** $2.60/credit (list is $3.00; \ ~13% discount)
- **Unused credits:** Roll over within the contract year; do not carry across years
- **Storage pricing:** $23/TB/month, capped at current rate for contract duration
- **Marketplace listings:** Excluded from commit; billed separately at list

## Workloads Covered

Primarily Analytics/Data Engineering workloads:
- `PROD_ANALYTICS` warehouse (XL, auto-suspend 5 min)
- `PROD_REPORTING` warehouse (M, always-on during business hours)
- `STAGING` environment warehouses

## Legal Notes

- IP and data ownership: customer retains all rights to data stored in Snowflake
- Snowflake's DPA (Data Processing Addendum) v3.2 is incorporated by reference
- Governing law: California
- Dispute resolution: binding arbitration unless either party seeks injunctive relief

## Redlines Accepted by Snowflake

- Extended credit rollover from 30 days to end-of-contract-year
- Added right to audit Snowflake's credit calculation methodology
- Removed "benchmark clause" that would have prohibited publishing performance comparisons

## Renewal Planning

Renewal window opens ~2027-02. Usage is currently tracking at about 65% of annual credit budget — either we grow into it or negotiate down at renewal. See `../budget.md` for overall data platform spend allocation.
