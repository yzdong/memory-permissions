# Corporate Travel Agency Agreement — Navan (formerly TripActions)

**Status:** Active  
**Effective:** 2024-01-01  
**Term:** 2 years (expires 2025-12-31)  
**Owner:** Tom Reyes  
**Navan AE:** Priscilla Tam

## Agreement Type

SaaS + service agreement covering:
1. Navan platform (travel booking SaaS)
2. Navan Expense (expense management)
3. Navan's travel agency services (agent assist, duty of care)

## Pricing Structure

- **Platform fee:** $0 (covered by supplier commissions per Navan's standard model)
- **Expense module:** $10/active user/month (billed based on users who submit at least one report)
- **Agent assist calls:** First 30 calls/month included; $15/call above threshold

Total estimated cost: ~$18–22K/year based on ~150 active expense users monthly.

## Travel Policy Integration

Our travel policy is configured directly in Navan (approval thresholds, preferred hotels, preferred airlines). Any policy changes must go through Finance + People Ops sign-off; Tom owns the Navan admin credentials.

Current out-of-policy booking rate: ~8%. Target is < 5%. Review with Priscilla at next QBR.

## Duty of Care Features

- Emergency notification contacts: Tom Reyes + HR Business Partner on file
- International SOS integration: active
