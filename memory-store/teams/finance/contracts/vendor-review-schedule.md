## Process Notes

- All contracts > $250K TCV require CFO signature regardless of department
- Contracts should be loaded into ContractSafe within 5 business days of execution
- Finance review required for any vendor requesting PII access — loop in Security and Legal
- Budget impact of renewals: reference `../budget.md` for current fiscal year tooling envelopes

## Review Cadence

- **Weekly:** Tom reviews open items and upcoming 30-day deadlines
- **Monthly:** Finance team sync on contract status (first Tuesday of month)
- **Quarterly:** CFO review of top-10 vendors by spend
