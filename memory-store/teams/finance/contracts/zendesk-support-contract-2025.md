# Zendesk Support Contract — 2025

**Status:** Active  
**Term:** 2025-06-01 to 2026-05-31  
**Owner:** Customer Success + Finance  
**Renewal Decision Deadline:** 2026-03-01  

## Scope

Zendesk Suite Professional for the customer support team. Includes:
- Help Center (public-facing knowledge base)
- Ticket management with SLA tracking
- Talk (voice support add-on — 5 seats)
- Explore (reporting and analytics)

## Agent Count

35 agents currently. Support is growing; CS leadership has flagged potential need for 45–50 agents by renewal. Finance to incorporate in renewal scenario modeling.

## Issues Flagged This Term

- **SLA breach in Q3 2025:** Zendesk had a 4-hour partial outage affecting ticket routing. We invoked the SLA credit clause and received a 3-day service credit. Documented in `../vendor-incidents/zendesk-q3-2025-outage.md`.
- **Explore performance:** Several CS analysts report slow dashboard loads for reports over 90-day windows. Raised with Zendesk TAM — they suggested archival policy changes, not a satisfying answer. Consider this a downgrade risk.

## Renewal Considerations

1. **Evaluate Intercom vs Freshdesk vs Zendesk:** CS team prefers Zendesk UI but pricing has crept up. Finance wants a competitive quote before auto-renewing.
2. **Annual escalation:** Current contract has CPI + 2% escalator — that's higher than we want. Push for fixed 3% cap.
3. **Talk add-on:** Low utilization (~60% of seats active monthly). May drop to 3 seats at renewal.
4. **AI features:** Zendesk Advanced AI is being pushed by their sales team. Get a demo but don't commit without CS leadership sign-off and a proper pilot plan.
