# Vendor Payment Terms Policy

**Owner:** Accounts Payable  
**Effective:** February 1, 2025  
**Last Reviewed:** 2025-09-05

---

## Standard Payment Terms

Our default payment terms for all new vendors are **Net 30** from invoice date, unless an exception is granted. This applies regardless of what the vendor's standard terms state — our MSA or SOW governs.

## Negotiated Exceptions

Vendors may be granted non-standard terms under the following conditions:

| Scenario | Allowable Terms | Approver |
|---|---|---|
| Early payment discount ≥ 2% | Net 10 or earlier | Finance VP |
| Strategic / sole-source vendor | Net 45 or Net 60 (vendor request) | CFO |
| Startup / small vendor (< 20 employees) | Net 15 at AP discretion | AP Manager |
| SLA-backed services (hosting, payroll) | As per contract, up to Net 7 | Finance VP |

## Paying Before Invoice Due Date

Early payment outside an approved early-pay-discount arrangement is **not permitted** without Finance VP sign-off. This preserves cash positioning and working capital targets.

## Late Payments

We aim to pay 98%+ of invoices on time. If a vendor is past due:

1. AP will flag in the weekly late-payment report.
2. Budget owner is notified to confirm there is no dispute.
3. Payment is expedited in the next available batch run.

Persistent late payments due to internal approval delays are tracked and fed back to the responsible team's Finance BP quarterly.

## Vendor-Initiated Disputes

If a vendor contacts you directly about a late or incorrect payment, redirect them to `ap@company.internal`. Do not make any verbal commitments about payment timing.

## Currency

All payments default to **USD**. Non-USD payments require Treasury involvement. See `fx-hedging-policy.md` for guidance on currency exposure management.
