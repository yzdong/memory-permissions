# Vendor Payment Terms Policy

**Owner:** Accounts Payable / Treasury  
**Effective:** January 2026

## Standard Terms

Unless a contract specifies otherwise, our default payment term for all vendors is **Net 30** from the invoice date. Accounts Payable will not pay earlier than Net 30 without Treasury approval — holding float is a deliberate cash management strategy.

## Early Pay Discounts

If a vendor offers an early pay discount, AP must evaluate it and escalate to Treasury if the discount rate annualizes to above **8%**. Most 2/10 Net 30 terms (2% discount for payment within 10 days) meet this threshold and should generally be taken.

To flag an early pay opportunity: email `treasury@company.internal` with the invoice attached and subject `EARLY PAY - [Vendor] - [Discount Terms]`.

## Extended Terms Requests

For large or strategic vendors, Treasury may negotiate extended terms (Net 45, Net 60, or longer). Requests from the business should go through the procurement team (`teams/procurement/vendor-onboarding.md`), not directly to AP.

Any agreement to terms beyond Net 60 requires CFO approval and must be reflected in the executed contract.

## Exceptions: Immediate Payment

Certain vendor categories are paid on receipt or within 5 days by agreement:
- Legal firms on retainer (per retainer agreements)
- Regulatory filing agents (to avoid late fees)
- Utilities and critical infrastructure providers

If a new vendor requests immediate payment terms, AP must route to Treasury for review. Agreeing to these terms verbally or informally without Treasury sign-off is a policy violation.

## International Vendors

- Payment currency follows the contract. Where not specified, USD is default.
- Wire transfers for international vendors require dual authorization from AP Supervisor + Treasury.
- Intermediary bank fees are borne by the company unless the contract states otherwise. Use the preferred wire template in NetSuite to minimize routing costs.
- For FX implications of large cross-border payments, see [fx-hedging-policy.md](fx-hedging-policy.md).

## Vendor Disputes and Holds

Vendors placed on payment hold by AP or Legal cannot be paid regardless of invoice age. The hold register is maintained by AP and reviewed weekly. If you believe a hold is outdated, contact `ap@company.internal` — do not route around it.

## Audit Trail

Every payment must have a linked invoice, PO (where applicable), and approval record in NetSuite. Payments without full documentation will be flagged in the quarterly AP audit and escalated to the Controller.
