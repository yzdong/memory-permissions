# Expense Policy — FY2026

**Owner:** Finance Operations  
**Effective:** January 1, 2026  
**Review Cycle:** Annual (next review: Q4 2026)

---

## Purpose

This document defines allowable business expenses, approval thresholds, and submission requirements for all full-time employees and contractors billing to company cost centers.

## General Principles

- Expenses must be **business-necessary** and **reasonable**.
- Personal expenses — even if incidentally incurred during travel — are not reimbursable.
- Receipts required for all line items above **$25 USD**.
- Expenses submitted more than **45 calendar days** after incurrence will not be reimbursed without VP-level exception. See `reimbursement-cutoffs.md`.

## Expense Categories & Limits

| Category | Per-Transaction Limit | Monthly Soft Cap | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Meals (solo) | $60 | $300 | No alcohol on solo meals |
| Meals (client) | $150/head | — | Pre-approval if >6 attendees |
| Ground transport | $120 | — | Uber Black disallowed |
| Software / SaaS | $500 | — | IT procurement review required above this |
| Office supplies | $200 | — | — |
| Training / conf. | $2,000 | — | Manager approval required |

## Out-of-Policy Items

The following are **never reimbursable** regardless of approval level:

- Fines, penalties, traffic tickets
- Gym memberships (outside approved wellness stipend)
- Gift cards purchased for personal use
- First-class flights for domestic routes under 4 hours

## Submission Process

1. Upload receipts to **Expensify** within 30 days of purchase.
2. Tag the correct cost center — contact `#finance-ops` if unsure.
3. Manager approves in Expensify; Finance spot-checks 15% of all submissions.
4. Reimbursement processed on the **2nd and 4th Friday** of each month.
