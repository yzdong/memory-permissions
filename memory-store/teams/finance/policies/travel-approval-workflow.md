# Travel Approval Workflow

**Owner:** Finance Operations + People Ops  
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-14  
**Applies To:** All employees booking company-funded travel

---

## Overview

All domestic and international travel requires pre-approval before booking. This workflow defines who approves, what triggers escalation, and how to handle urgent trips.

## Approval Tiers

### Domestic Travel (US, Canada, Mexico)

| Estimated Trip Cost | Approver |
|---|---|
| < $1,500 | Direct manager |
| $1,500 – $5,000 | Manager + Department Head |
| > $5,000 | Manager + Department Head + Finance VP |

### International Travel

All international trips require **Finance VP sign-off**, regardless of cost. Allow **5 business days** for approval — do not book before approval is confirmed in writing.

## How to Submit a Travel Request

1. Open a **TripRequest** ticket in the internal portal (link in Notion under Finance > Travel).
2. Attach a draft itinerary and purpose-of-travel statement (2–3 sentences is fine).
3. Tag your manager and cc `finance-travel@company.internal`.
4. Once approved, book through **TravelPerk** using your corporate card — see `corporate-card-issuance.md`.

## Urgent / Last-Minute Travel

If travel must be booked within 48 hours:

- Email your manager and `finance-travel@company.internal` directly.
- Include a one-line business justification in the subject line.
- Manager can grant verbal approval, but written confirmation must follow within 24 hours.
- Finance reserves the right to flag last-minute bookings for audit.

## Hotel & Airfare Standards

- **Airfare:** Economy for all flights. Business class permitted for routes > 8 hours *only* with VP approval.
- **Hotels:** Up to $280/night in Tier 1 cities (NYC, SF, London, Singapore), $200 elsewhere.
- Airbnb and similar rentals require Finance approval and a business justification.
