# Corporate Card Issuance Policy

**Owner:** Finance Operations  
**Last Updated:** 2025-10-01  
**Applies To:** Full-time employees and designated contractors

---

## Who Is Eligible

Corporate cards (issued through Brex) are provided to:

- All Director-level and above employees (automatic issuance on hire)
- Employees who travel more than **4 times per year** on company business
- Team leads with recurring vendor payment responsibilities
- Any employee with written Manager + Finance approval

Contractors are **not** eligible for physical cards; they may be issued virtual single-use cards for specific purchases upon request.

## Card Limits

| Level | Monthly Spend Limit |
|---|---|
| IC / Analyst | $2,500 |
| Senior / Lead | $5,000 |
| Manager | $10,000 |
| Director+ | $25,000 |

Limits can be temporarily increased with Finance VP approval — submit a request to `#finance-ops` with a business justification.

## Requesting a Card

1. Submit a **CardRequest** in the internal Finance portal.
2. Manager must approve the request.
3. Finance Operations reviews and provisions within 3 business days.
4. Physical cards are mailed to home address on file; virtual cards are available immediately.

## Responsibilities of Cardholders

- All charges must be categorized and receipts attached in Brex within **15 days** of transaction.
- Cardholders are personally liable for any charges that cannot be verified as business expenses.
- Do **not** share card credentials with anyone, including direct reports.
- Lost/stolen cards must be reported to Finance within 2 hours of discovery.

## Revocation

Cards will be revoked upon:

- Employment termination (automatic, same day as offboarding)
- Two consecutive months of missing expense categorization
- Confirmed policy violation (see `expense-policy-2026.md`)
